Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19 January 2026

Mamba Security values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with South African laws, including the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

This Privacy Policy explains how Mamba Security collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us. Please read this privacy policy carefully. By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this privacy policy.

1

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Contact us through forms, phone calls, or email
  • Request security assessments or quotes
  • Subscribe to our newsletters or updates
  • Become a client or use our services

This may include your name, email address, phone number, physical address, identity number, and other contact details.

Non-Personal Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Operating system and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Cookies and tracking technology data
2

Purpose of Collecting Information

We collect and use your information for the following legitimate purposes:

Service Delivery

To respond to inquiries, provide requested security services, and maintain client relationships

Communication

To send important updates, security alerts, and newsletters (with your consent)

Improvement

To enhance website functionality, user experience, and service quality

Compliance

To comply with South African laws, regulations, and security industry requirements

3

Sharing Information

Mamba Security does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

Trusted Service Providers

With partners who assist us in website operations, payment processing, or service delivery, under strict confidentiality agreements

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or government authorities, including law enforcement and regulatory bodies

Protection Rights

To protect the rights, property, or safety of Mamba Security, our clients, or the public

4

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Enhance website functionality and performance
  • Remember user preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Provide personalized content where applicable

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.

5

Data Security

Technical Measures

Encryption, firewalls, secure servers, and regular security assessments

Access Controls

Strict access limitations and authentication protocols for staff

Organizational Measures

Staff training, privacy policies, and regular security audits

Data Retention

Personal information is only retained as long as necessary for legal or business purposes

While we implement reasonable security measures, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information to the best of our ability.

6

Your Rights Under POPIA

As a South African resident, you have specific rights under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA):

Right to Access

Request access to the personal information we hold about you

Right to Correction

Request correction or updating of inaccurate personal data

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information under certain circumstances

Right to Object

Object to processing of your personal information

Exercise Your Rights

7

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites for your convenience or reference. Mamba Security is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of these third-party websites.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

8

Children's Privacy

Age Restriction

Our website and services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under 13.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

9

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. The updated effective date will be displayed at the top of this page.

Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page regularly for updates.

For significant changes, we may notify you via email or prominent website notice.
