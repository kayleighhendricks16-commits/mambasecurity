Corporate Guarding

Following a full risk assessment of a potential clients premises, we customise a solution that best suits our clients needs. Guarding has proved to be an effective solution in securing our clients premises by incorporating access control, site patrols and the use of various state of the art technologies.

Client specific Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) are compiled in conjunction with our clients. Security officers receive training on those procedures. Following a comprehensive employment process which includes background checks, psychometric testing and emotional stability assessment, amongst other things, security officers best suited to that specific client are identified and deployed.

A guarding manager is allocated to each client and carries out regular formal meetings and site inspections in order to implement and maintain superior service delivery standards. A number of employee reward programs and incentives are in place for our staff, ensuring that they are recognized for their dedication and commitment to our clients.

Road Closures

Mamba Guarding has been the service provider of choice, currently servicing the majority of road closures in our footprint. A range of vehicle barriers and access control facilities are available for security enclosures, allowing customisation of your security enclosure according to your community's needs.

Build your security plan through a variety of products including pedestrian barriers, spiked vehicle barriers, speed bumps, traffic islands, card readers, turnstiles and biometrics. Our teams are able to create a custom CCTV solution for your enclosure, whether you require cameras for site overview, or Smart Camera Network with License Plate Recognition (LPR).

Schools Security

Effective strategies are implemented to secure the entire premises, with special attention being paid to high risk areas like admin and computer centres.

AD HOC Security

Over and above the specialised guarding services provided by Mamba, we are also able to assist with ADHOC guarding services, in order to ensure that your party, corporate function, wedding etc goes off without any security related glitches. We can provide competent ADHOC guarding services for anything from a few hours to a few weeks.

Mamba's ADHOC guards are posted on sites fully equipped with batons, handcuffs, panic system and handheld radio communication with our locally based control centre.

Our large fleet of armed response vehicles provide immediate backup for our guards in the event of any emergency situation.
