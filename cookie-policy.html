Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 19 January 2026

Mamba Security uses cookies and similar technologies on our website to improve user experience, analyze website traffic, and provide certain functionalities. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices.

This policy works alongside our Privacy Policy to explain how we handle your data.

Understanding Cookies

Cookies help make your online experience better by remembering your preferences and helping us understand how our website is used. This policy explains everything you need to know about cookies on the Mamba Security website.

1

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are stored on your device when you visit websites

Technical Definition

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Storage

Cookies are stored locally on your device by your web browser

Memory

They remember your actions and preferences over time

Performance

Help websites load faster and remember your settings

Different types of cookies serve different purposes, from essential website functionality to analytics and personalization.

2

Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies for various purposes. Each type serves a specific function to enhance your browsing experience.

Essential Cookies

Required

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off.

Purpose

  • Enable secure login and authentication
  • Allow form submissions and security features
  • Maintain user sessions and navigation
  • Prevent fraudulent use of login credentials

Functionality

Essential cookies do not store personal information and are typically set in response to actions you take, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms.

These cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for basic website operation.

Performance & Analytics Cookies

Optional

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Purpose

  • Track page visits and user pathways
  • Measure session duration and bounce rates
  • Identify popular content and features
  • Understand user behavior patterns

Functionality

These cookies collect aggregated information that helps us improve how our website works. They don't collect information that identifies you personally.

Analytics Services

We may use services like Google Analytics to help analyze how users use the site. These services use cookies to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information.

Functionality Cookies

Optional

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

Purpose

  • Remember user preferences (language, region)
  • Store layout and display preferences
  • Enable personalized features
  • Remember login details (if requested)

Functionality

These cookies may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages. They remember choices you make to improve your experience.

Example: If you select a preferred language, a cookie will remember this choice for your next visit.

Third-Party Cookies

External

These cookies are set by external services that appear on our pages.

Purpose

  • Social media integration (sharing buttons)
  • Embedded videos and content
  • Advertising and retargeting
  • Analytics and performance tools

Functionality

Third-party cookies are subject to the privacy policies of the respective third parties. We do not control these cookies.

Important Notice

Third-party cookies are governed by the privacy policies of the respective third parties. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.

4

Your Cookie Choices

You have control over the cookies stored on your device. Here's how you can manage them:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser.

Common Browser Controls:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Cookie Management

You can choose to accept or reject cookies. Most browsers also allow you to delete cookies that have already been stored.

Accept All Cookies

Allow all cookies for the best website experience

Block All Cookies

Prevent all cookies from being stored (may break some website features)

Selective Blocking

Block third-party cookies while allowing essential cookies

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Essential Cookies

Disabling these will prevent the website from functioning properly

Performance Cookies

We won't be able to gather data to improve our website

Functionality Cookies

Website won't remember your preferences on return visits

Third-Party Cookies

External features like social sharing may not work

By continuing to use our website without changing your cookie settings, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw this consent at any time by changing your browser settings.
