Security Tips

Practical security habits make all the difference. Follow these tips to protect yourself, your family, and your property.

Personal Safety

  • Always stay aware of your surroundings
  • Avoid sharing travel plans or personal details publicly
  • Trust your instincts — leave if something feels unsafe
  • Keep emergency contacts easily accessible

Home Safety

  • Lock doors, windows, and gates at all times
  • Install motion-activated lighting and alarms
  • Keep valuables out of sight and store important items securely
  • Test alarm systems regularly

Vehicle Safety

  • Lock your car at all times, even for short stops
  • Park in well-lit, busy areas
  • Keep valuables hidden from view
  • Be cautious when approaching or leaving your vehicle

Community Awareness

  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Know your neighbours and encourage cooperation
  • Participate in neighbourhood watch programs
  • Support vulnerable members of the community

These tips form the first line of defense — combine them with professional security for complete protection.
